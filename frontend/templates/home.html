Common Diseases

Learn more about common diseases affecting pets and strays. this cards display some common disease in cat and dog

Conjunctivitis (Pink Eye)

Inflammation caused by bacterial or viral infections, resulting in redness and discharge. Treatment includes vet-prescribed eye drops and proper cleaning.

Ringworm

A contagious fungal infection causing circular hair loss and scaly skin. Requires antifungal treatment and proper hygiene.

Scabies (Sarcoptic Mange)

Caused by mites, leading to itching and crusty skin. Symptoms include redness and hair loss. Treatment includes medicated baths and mite-killing medications.

Flea Allergy Dermatitis

Severe itching and redness caused by flea saliva. Regular flea prevention and anti-itch treatments help manage symptoms and prevent secondary infections.

Gingivitis

Gum inflammation caused by plaque buildup. Symptoms include red gums and bad breath. Professional dental cleaning and regular brushing help prevent progression.

Mange

A mite infestation leading to extreme itching, hair loss, and skin lesions. Sarcoptic mange is highly contagious; demodectic mange affects immunocompromised animals.
